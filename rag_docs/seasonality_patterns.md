# Seasonality Patterns & Business Calendar — NovaTech Solutions Inc.

---

## Annual Revenue Seasonality

NovaTech's revenue follows a predictable back-half weighted pattern:

| Quarter | Revenue Mix | Key Driver |
|---|---|---|
| Q1 | 21% of annual | Slow procurement; new logos from Q4 pipeline begin ramping |
| Q2 | 24% of annual | Sales velocity picks up; SKO impact on pipeline |
| Q3 | 26% of annual | Strong renewal cycle; conference drives awareness |
| Q4 | 29% of annual | Strongest quarter; year-end budget flush by customers, renewal concentration |

**Month-specific patterns:**
- **January:** Softest month; customer IT freezes from holidays, procurement restarts
- **March:** End-of-quarter push; Sales team accelerates closes; highest booking month in Q1
- **July/August:** Slower due to summer vacations; enterprise buyers less responsive
- **September:** Q3 close push; strong booking month; implementations begin that will recognize revenue in Q4
- **November:** Strong bookings as customers finalize year-end budget spending
- **December:** Revenue recognition high from November closes; bookings soft (holiday season) but renewals peak

## Expense Seasonality Calendar

### January
- Infrastructure: Normal
- Salaries: Normal (prior year merit ended; new merit effective April)
- Legal/Audit: Audit fieldwork begins (expect +15-20% vs monthly avg)
- Marketing: Low (Q1 digital only)

### February
- No significant seasonal patterns
- Agency retainer invoices can arrive for January work (billing lag)

### March
- T&E: Slight increase (end-of-quarter customer visits)
- No major events

### April ⚡ HIGH VARIANCE MONTH
- **T&E: SPIKE +50-60%** — Sales Kickoff (SKO) typically held first two weeks of April
- Salaries: +3-4% step change from annual merit increase (effective April 1)
- Marketing: Moderate (Q2 digital campaign launch)

### May
- T&E: Normalizes after SKO
- No major patterns

### June
- Marketing: Moderate (end of H1 digital campaign)
- T&E: Slight increase (CAB meeting and end-of-quarter customer events)

### July ⚡ MARKETING SPIKE MONTH
- **Marketing: BEGINS RAMP** — H2 brand campaign and user conference preparation begins
- Infrastructure: May see provisioning costs for new Q2 logos going live

### August
- **Marketing: ELEVATED** — Conference sponsorship payments due, campaign in full swing
- General: Vacation season; some purchase orders delayed to September

### September ⚡ MARKETING PEAK
- **Marketing: PEAK** — User conference (NovaSummit) occurs; largest single-month marketing event
- T&E: Elevated for conference attendance
- Infrastructure: Q3 implementations going live can cause temporary spike

### October
- Infrastructure: Often elevated if new implementations went live in September
- Marketing: Drops sharply post-conference
- T&E: Customer advisory board meeting

### November ⚡ LEGAL/AUDIT MONTH
- **Legal: ELEVATED** — Year-end audit preparation, contract renewals with legal review
- Commissions: Often elevated as Q4 deals close early
- Revenue: Strong (renewal concentration)

### December
- Legal/Audit: Audit fees peak (year-end procedures)
- T&E: Holiday events, year-end customer dinners
- Revenue: Strong (year-end customer budget spend, renewals)
